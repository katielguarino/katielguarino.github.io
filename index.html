About Me

I'm a professional in Industrial/Organizational Psychology currently working as a Senior Consultant on a people analytics team at EY. I do my best work when I can blend my I/O Psychology background and passions for data analysis and the employee experience. I work on projects for Fortune 500 companies, collaborating with stakeholders, teammates, and leaders to turn HR data into actionable insights and interventions that bring about lasting change.

I am highly self-motivated and strive for continual growth both personally and professionally. My recent self-development projects have included self-directed courses in sentiment analysis using R, and joining data in SQL, as well as learning git and creating this webpage! On a more personal note, in my spare time I enjoy baking and reading.

Assessment Development

Types of assessments I've helped develop include job knowledge, computer adaptive personality, work simulations, structured interviews, and gamified assessments.

Training

I've developed and delievered trainings on assessment development, interviewer and assessor training, building trust, conflict management, and wellbeing.

People Analytics

I'm formally trained and experienced in regression, psychometrics, and adverse impact analysis. I'm learning machine learning and NLP. Software/scripting capabilities: R, Power BI, Tableau, SPSS, SQL.

Quantitative Research

I'm Human Subjects Certified (CITI) and experienced in conducting quantitative research, including content and criterion validity studies, with small and large samples (5000+).
